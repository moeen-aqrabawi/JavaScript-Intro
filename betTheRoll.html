You and the computer will roll two dice. The results of both rolls for each player will display below. Click 'OK.' Or the 'Enter' button on your keyboard at the prompt to continue rolling the dice. First to reach 100 or more wins! You get double the points for rolling the same number! In case of a tie, no points will be awarded. Tip: Scroll to the bottom of the page, so that the table is in the middle of your window when you press the Roll Dice button.

The dice rolls between 1 and 6.
